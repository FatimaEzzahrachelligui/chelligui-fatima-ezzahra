Shape: Crochet hooks come in different shapes, each serving a specific purpose:

- Inline Hooks: These hooks have a throat (the groove where the yarn rests) that is in line with the shaft of the hook.

- Tapered Hooks: Tapered hooks have a gradually sloping throat, which allows the yarn to glide smoothly

- Ergonomic Hooks: These hooks have a specially designed handle that provides a comfortable grip, reducing strain on the hand and wrist.Ergonomic hooks are ideal for people with hand or joint issues .

Sizes: Crochet hooks come in various sizes, denoted by letters, numbers, or millimeter measurements.

Material: Crochet hooks are typically made from various materials, including metal, plastic, wood, or bamboo.
